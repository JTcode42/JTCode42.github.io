An Assault on the Majority: The Despicable Mishandling of Public Offerings

Robinhood had promised a free option for regular people to trade. The service makes plenty of sense in practice, but when special interest groups and hedge fund managers dip their toes in the corporate soup of stock trading things get a bit spicy.
