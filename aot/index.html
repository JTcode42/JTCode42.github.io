Natural Language Processing

In this analysis, I use the Penn Treebank Word Tokenizer to perform natural language processing on the dialogue in the manga, "Attack on Titan". Qualitative summary statistics such as word frequency, correlation, and averaging occured in the analysis.

Manga and Anime: Crossover Analysis

Retrieving the dialogue from the manga is significantly more complex computationally so I performed my analysis on the script from the anime. This choice was stricly based on the availability of the data. I read the manga and I am aware there is more content in the manga to analyze. Unfortunately, the qualitative data in this analysis is strictly what is being said in the anime.

Tokenized Data

The script is processed by assigning each word to a number. This way counts and correlations are easier to determine.

Term Document Matrix

A term document matrix is used specifically to understand the relationship words have to one another. This informationincludes what words appear near each other and the frequency that these combinations occur.

Word Frequency

This data is central to this analysis due to the type of analysis I am performing. The words that have the highest frequency generally paint a mental picture of the type of narrative that is being told.

Filtering

A big part of natural language processing is filtering characters such as (',",;,=,+,&,#,etc.) to ensure that words that include these characters are still included in the count, alongside the same words that occur without the problematic punctuation.

How is Attack on Titan making the reader feel?

This article is a mix between scholarly research, personal interpretation, and natural language processing. I will touch on all of these methods to ensure completeness and an unbias approach. Like many literary works, Attack on Titan is a rather interpretable piece of literature. There are many different interpretations and no one interpretation is the "right" one. Hajime Isayama (the author), may have the answers this research is attempting to uncover, but for now with the anime expected to conclude in the winter of 2022, Isayama is keeping a low profile in terms of giving away any information that may enter spoiler territory. With all of that being said, let's analyze away!!!
